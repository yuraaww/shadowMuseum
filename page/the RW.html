皇家威廉號

  此船的歷史記載只有斷簡殘篇。 目前找到的資料顯示，這艘船是仿照1670年建造「太子號」改造而成。 自1719年下水到1831年解體，可以說是18世紀英國海軍歷史上著名的船隻，它的船體是取用冬天的木材並用火焦乾處理過，一般的利器不能劈破。

  1782年，這艘船當護衛艦，參加魁北克戰役，提供救護，執行醫療任務。 於1831年因老朽而解體。




The "Royal William"

  Few records have been found of the history of this ship.  Sources gathered up to now show that she was remodeled out of the "Prince", a vessel built in 1670.  The timbers from which this ship was made seem to have been collected in winter and dried with fire to such a degree that common cutting tools could not split them.  In 1728, the ship took part in a sea battle near the harbor of Quebec as an escort, offering life-saving and medical services.  In 1831, because of a general decrepit condition she was broken up.
