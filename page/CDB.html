清朝中國艦

  這艘船是比利時最著名的比例模型建造師鮑威爾斯先生所建造的。鮑威爾斯先生曾在歐洲模型船比賽中榮膺冠軍。他的船以表現栩栩如生聞名。 建造本船之前，鮑威爾斯先生曾在中國停留長達廿個月，研究中國船，尤其是帆船的結構，也仔細的研究過中國水手的衣裝和造型。 鮑威爾斯生性細心，講求精確。整條船工作的完成耗時達三年半。他細心製造才使本館能夠陳列出這艘卓越、精緻及美麗的中國帆船。

  這艘中國帆船建造於1930年代，她是一件卓越的藝術精品。船身由整塊原木做成，甲板上帆椼索具配備齊全，並放置30位左右的中國水手，人體微細，全由手雕。




Ching Dynasty Battleship

  The ship was built by Mr.Pauwels, one of Belgium's most famous scale-model builders, who won championships in model ship contests in Europe.  His models are renowned for their fidelity of expression.  Before building the ship, Mr.Pauwels spent more than 20 months in China, studying the structure of Chinese vessels, especially junks.  He also minutely studied the costume and physical features of Chinese sailors.

  Mr.Pauwels was meticulous and very particular about authenticity, so it took him three and half years to finish the whole project of building this Ching Dynasty Battleship.  His painstaking care accounts for the rarity, exquisiteness and beauty of the Chinese ship now displayed in this museum.

  As an unique piece of art, this model vessel was built in the 1930's.  Made of a single piece of wood, the hull is fully rigged and staffed on deck with about 30 miniature hand-carved figures in the form of early Chinese sailors.
