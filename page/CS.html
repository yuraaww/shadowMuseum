貨櫃船及貨櫃拖車

  貨櫃船是現代貨物輪中運輸效率最高的船隻，她的運載方式是先將貨物裝在統一規格的貨櫃箱中，再集中於港口裝船(船上有寬敞的甲板和船艙)。  除了可以將貨物提早運抵目的地，縮短運送時間外，還可以增加貨物的運輸量。  本貨櫃船模型比例為1:500。

  貨運拖車是一種特製汽車，專供拖運貨櫃船眾多貨櫃之車輛，既迅速又方便。




Container Ship and Container Carrier Truck

  Container ships have the highest efficiency among all cargo vessels for shipping.  The transportation system utilizing the container eship involves packing cargo, putting it into standardized containers, and transmitting it by truck to the port for shipping.  Having spacious decks and compartments, a container ship allows a large volume of cargo each trip and a shorter time delivering the cargo to the port of destination.  The model on display here is built in the scale of 1 to £500.

  The container carrier truck is fast and convenient, designed eespecially for transmitting containers to and from the port.
