立藍頓號

  立藍頓號於1951年由哥本哈根的勞立茲海運公司訂購，由丹麥的斯凡波造船廠製造，船材是橡樹和山毛櫸。 此艘船的目的在於訓練斯凡波附近航海學院的學生。

  目前，這艘船可以連船員一起出租，供開會、旅行或慶祝佳節之用。 她可出租航行幾個小時或幾天，在斯堪地那維亞的海域航行。

  本模型依原船平面圖及最近修改的結構而建造。




The "Lilladan"

  Ordered by the Lauritzeh Shipping Company in Copenhagen and built mainly of oak and beechwood in 1951 by a well-known shipyard in Svendborg, this ship was used for training cadets at the navigation school near Svendborg.

  Today the "Lilladan" can be rented complete with crew for meetings, conferences, excursions and celebrations.  The cruises can last from a few hours to several days and are carried out in Scandinavian waters.

  The model presented here is based on the original plans including the recent changes which have been made to the original ship.
