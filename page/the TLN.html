多羅納斯號

  這艘船是在1823年8月13日啟航，曾在傑爾興上校的命令下參加西班牙戰役，駐紮過巴塞隆納，也曾遠及卡丁茲，有助於當地海港轟炸設施。 1832年在波瑞斯特，經過一連串徹底檢查後，換新了內外的箍條最前面的部位和艦橋部份的木材，重做甲板的架構。 直到1836年，在馬丁尼加的皇家堡壘，又做了些修補。 1843年12月18日，特設的委員會認為此船已不值得在做修補。

本模型船是根據法國博物館內大型模型船製造的，比例為1:75。




The "Toulonnaise"

  Launched on August 13, 1823, this ship took part in the Spanish War under Major Joursin's command.  After being stationed at Barcelona for some time she sailed southward to Cadiz, where she helped in the shelling of the port facilities.

  In 1832 after a thorough check at Brest, all the girders, external and internal, the foremast and timbers of the bridge were changed.  The coverings of the deck were also redone.  In 1836 the ship was repaired again at Fort Royal in Martinique.  On December 18, 1843 an appointed committee judged that the ship was no longer worthy of further repairs.

  The model presented here was made in the scale of 1 to 75 on the basis of the data obtained from a big scale model kept in French museum.
