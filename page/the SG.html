西班牙大帆船

  這艘西班牙大帆船是一種新型的船隻，她於1607年下水，以取代古時候的帆船(carrack)。

  這種大帆船的船身比舊時的帆船更修長而優雅，其船首艛不再突出於前端，而是在被稱為鐵嘴的角狀突出物之後。




The "Spanish Galleon"

  This Spanish galleon was a new type of vessel launched in 1607 to replace the carrack.  Her hull was more slender and graceful than that of the carrack, with the forecastle no longer overhanging the bow but built behind a ram-like projection called the beak-head.
