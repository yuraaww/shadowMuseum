阿斯特拉伯號

  法國的一段砲列帆船軍艦，建於1811年，她的主要任務為測量地中海、黑海的海圖。 1822年曾航行世界一周，亦曾到南及探險，一直到1851年才退休。




The "Astrolabe"

  This is a French sailboat built in 1811.  Although she was equipped with battery guns, she was used mainly for surveying and making maps of the Mediterranean Sea and the Black Sea.  In 1822, she completed a round-the-world trip.  Afterward, she cruised and explored the South Polar Regions and did not retire until 1851.
