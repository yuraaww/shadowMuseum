長信輪

長榮海運公司所屬15000噸級高性能多用途快速乾貨船。

1983年，長榮海運將15000噸的雜貨船長安輪(EVER SAEETY)改裝為貨櫃實習船長信輪(EVER TRUST)， 由日本尾道造船廠負責改裝，於1984年3月底完工。

長信輪上配裝了當時最新型的航海儀器及輪機系統，並且所有的儀器皆有兩套， 一套是供船舶正常航行用，一套專供學生實習操作用，並提供學生設備完善的讀書及休憩空間， 長信輪每次可同時搭載二百名師生上船實習。

長榮海運為海事學生所提供的實習服務，非但完全免費，也未要求學生在畢業後需至長榮服務， 完全是無私的以提升國內的海事教育為主。 長信輪在1991年完成其階段性任務，期間提供了數千名學子上船實習的機會。

1996年，長信輪停泊於大阪時遭受火損，同年年底，於廣東番禹軋鋼廠解體。

本模型船比例為1：100。




The "Ever Trust"

  This is a high-speed, supremely functional and multi-purpose dry cargo ship.  Weighing 15,000 tons, she belongs to the Evergreen Marine Corporation.  In 1983, the Ever Safety, which was a steamship with fifteen thousand tons, was refitted into a containership and renamed the Ever Trust by a Japanese shipyard and the whole remodeling was completed in March 1984.  The Ever Trust was equipped with two sets of the latest navigation facility, including maritime scopes and turbine machines.  One set was for regular navigation and the other was for student practical training.Besides, she provided students with comfortable space for studying and recreation.  This ship carries 200 teachers and students each voyage.  Aimed at the upgrading maritime education with altruism, the Evergreen Marine Corporation provided maritime students with free practical training at school and job opportunities after graduation.  During her active term, this containership provided chances for thousands of students for practical training.  In 1991, the Ever Trust completed her programmed mission, and in 1996, she was damaged by fire at Osaka and di smant led at a factory in Kaundon at the end of the year.
