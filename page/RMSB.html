布列顛尼亞號

  布列顛尼亞號是英國Cunard公司為航行大西洋於1840年建造。 Cunard公司所屬尚有「瑪麗皇后號」及「諾曼第」，都是八萬噸級航行大西洋船客的先鋒。 「布列顛尼亞號」於1840年7月成為第一艘從英國來到美國東岸波士頓的客船，其橫渡地西洋旅遊的全盛時代持續至1930年間。

  此船長207呎，寬34呎，艙內高24呎4吋，總重1,156噸，排水2,050噸，裝有740匹馬力引擎，船速可達8.5哩，為三桅及橫椼且有風時能補助帆航行之兩側外輪式輪船(paddle side-wheeler)。

  「布列顛尼亞號」能載客115人及貨物225噸，上甲板有高級船員室、廚房、烘烤麵包和”牛棚” 小室可供新鮮牛奶；主甲板上有客艙和餐廳。 布列顛尼亞號由於建造堅固，因此在完成客輪服務後，曾賣給德軍作為巡洋艦使用。

  現今此模型展覽於停在美國西岸長堤的「瑪莉皇后號」上，由本館姊妹館—美國西岸洛杉磯海事博物館照原型為本館製造的。




RMS Britannia

  RMS Britannia was built by Cunard for sailing on the Atlantic.  All ships made by Cunard including "the Queen Mary" and "the Normandee" are pioneering passenger vessels on the Atlantic waters.  RMS Britannia arrived in Boston for the first time in July 1840. From that time to 1930 huge passenger vessels enjoyed prosperous business on Atlantic.

  RMS Britannia was 207 feet long at her keel, 34 feet inside her paddle-boxes, with a hold 24 feet 4 inches deep.  She had a gross tonnage of 1, 156 and 2,050 tons displacement.  Her engines were 740 hp capable of driving the ship at 8.5 knots.  She consumed 38 tons of coal a day.  She was rigged with three masts with her fore and main having two square cross-yards.

  She could carry 115 passengers and 225 tons of cargo.  The upper deck had officers' cabins, the galley, a bakery and a sort of padded cell called a "cow-house" for the ready supply of fresh milk.  The passenger cabins and dining saloons were on the main deck.  The ship was built so strong that, after her passenger service days were finished, she was sold to the German Navy for use as a cruiser.

  The model exhibited here is made according to the original model for the Tamkang Maritime Museum by our sister museum, The Los Angeles Maritime Museum.  The original model of this ship is kept on "the Queen Mary" in Long Beach, U. S. A.
