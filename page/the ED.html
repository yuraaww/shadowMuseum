奮勇號

  這是一艘英國式的木製雙桅船，她本是一艘商船，把英國的煤運到斯堪地那維亞；後來英國海軍用彭布羅克的名義買下她，改裝作為科學探測之用。 修改時，桅墙全然换新，船側加蓋橡木，舷牆加舷窗。 內部裝潢也自然而然的更新以配合當時的生活型態。

  1768年8月26日，這艘船從普利茅斯港出航時，船上的指揮是有名的詹姆士庫克，船上坐了94個人，包括船員、官員、學者及其家眷。

  從1768年到1780年，這艘船曾三次到南太平洋去探險，為英國獲得澳洲及紐西蘭等土地。

  本館陳列的模型船比例為1:50。




The "Endeavour"

  This is a British two-masted wooden ship (brigantine).  Before she was bought by the British Admiralty under the name of the Earl of Pembroke, she was a merchant ship carrying coal from England to Scandinavia.  The British Navy transformed her, making her suitable for scientific explorations.  Among other things the mast was fully changed, the careen was covered with planks of oak and along the bulwark many portholes were made.  Naturally, also the internal settings were changed to adapt her to modern civilian style of life.

  On August 26, 1768, when this ship set out from Plymouth under the command of the famous James Cook, on board were 94 people including crew, officers, scholars and their families.

  From 1768 to 1780, this ship explored the south Pacific three times, and thus acquired for England such territories as Australia and New Zealand.

  The model on display here was built in the scale of 1 to 50.
