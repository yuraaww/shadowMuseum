大和號

  大和號於1941年在日本製造。大和級包括四艘戰艦，但只完成大和號和武藏號。另外兩艘中的一艘信濃號改建為航空母艦，這幾艘都是世界最大的戰艦， 排水量為71,748公噸(72,900噸)，比華盛頓公約所准許的35,000噸要大二倍。有18英吋(460公釐)的大砲，比該公約所規定的16英吋(410公釐)還要大。 帶式鐵甲16英吋(410公釐)厚、長而尖細地包住火藥庫下方。主甲板外覆1-2英吋(25-50公釐)鋼板，保護性甲板則外覆7.8英吋(200公釐)厚的鋼板。 船底防護力量大。1943年並雙重加強武力以對抗飛機；把5英吋(127公釐)的大砲增加到24門；1英吋(25公釐)的機關槍增加到150枝。 雖然防衛措施極為講究，但兩艘戰艦都在美國轟炸機和魚雷飛機的攻擊下沉沒。大和號被10個魚雷和23顆炸彈所擊沉；武藏號則在11個魚雷和20顆炸彈圍攻下沉沒。

  大和號是日本艦隊的旗艦，她曾在1944年10月25日的撒馬戰役中擊沉美國航空母艦甘比灣號及另外三艘驅逐艦。 1945年4月7日在魚雷炸彈圍攻下沉沒於九州。




The "Yamato"

  "Yamato" was built in 1941.  The Yamato Class was to have included four units, but only "Yamato" and "Musashi" were completed.  One of the other two, "Shinano", was converted during construction to an aircraft carrier.  They were the largest battleships in the world with their displacement of 71,748t(72,900 tons), double the 35,000 tons allowed by the Washington Treaty.  The 18in(460mm) guns were also larger than the 16in(410mm) permitted by the Treaty.  The belt armor was 16in(410mm) thick and tapered down right underneath the magazines.  The main deck was armored with 1-2in(25-50mm) plates and the protective deck with plates 7.8in(200mm) thick.  The underwater protection was very effective.  Anti-aircraft armament was doubled in 1943; upping the number of 5in(127mm) guns to twenty-four, the number of 1in(25mm) machine guns was increased to 150.  Despite these elaborate defense measures, both units were the victims of attacks by American bombers and torpedo planes.  It took ten torpedoes and twenty-three bombs to sink "Yamato" and eleven torpedoes and twenty bombs to sink "Musashi".

  "Yamato" was the flagship of the Japanese fleet.  In the Battle of Samar on October 25, 1944, she sank the American aircraft carrier "Gambier Bay" and three destroyers.  On April 7, 1945, under attack from torpedo planes and bombers, she was sunk off Kyushu.
