藍鼻子號

  在航海史上，這艘船曾扮演過重要的角色。1921年從蘇格蘭下水，在紐芬蘭波浪洶湧的海上捕魚。 她的速度驚人，以後幾年在美國東海岸重要的賽舟會上皆奪冠。因她的速度比當時最快的快艇還要快而聲名遠播。 許多國家的錢幣和郵票都印有這艘船的圖片。

  1946年這艘船在海地附近觸礁，結束了令人尊敬的航海生涯。不過人們忘不了她，遂發起了募款活動， 1965年和藍鼻子相同的複製品建造完成下水，使藍鼻子號輝煌的歷史重現在人們的眼前。




The "Bluenose"

  This fore-and-aft sailboat played an important role in the history of navigation.  In 1921, she was launched from Scotland to fish in the rough waters off the Newfoundland coast.  Her exceptional speed soon became apparent.  In the years that followed this schooner won all the important races held along the American East Coast.  Her fame spread.  Consequently, the Bluenose as a fishing vessel that could outsail the fastest yacht of the day was seen on coins and stamps around the world.

  The schooner's venerable career abruptly ended in 1946 on a reef near Haiti.  Her memory, however, lives on.A sufficient fund was raised to build and launch an exact replica in 1965 to make the Bluenose a living symbol of her former glory.
