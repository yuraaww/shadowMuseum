安菲翁號

  這是一艘18世紀瑞典武裝快艇，為瑞典國王古斯塔夫斯三世娛樂而設計的。 可是在斯文克松戰役，她卻被當戰艦使用。 古斯塔夫斯三世在這艘船上指揮戰爭，打敗了俄羅斯帝國。

安菲翁號在斯德哥爾摩建造，1778年下水，她的盛名直到1850年仍然可聽見。 這艘船的原始平面圖是純線條和和諧的大傑作，是查普曼菲特烈設計的。 身為第一位海軍設計師，他設計船體時，不只是應用當時盛行的實驗資料，同時還依賴一絲不苟的數學知識。

在斯德哥爾摩的歷史博物館中存放了一艘安菲翁號的模型， 那個模型顯示出安菲翁號是一艘縱帆快艇(Schooner)，船身呈尖形，帶有海軍獨特的氣質。

本館陳列的這艘模型是依原始文獻以1:40的比例製造的。 我們盡心盡力，表現出她鍍金的裝飾，另外還用不同的木材以反映出她的配色及紋理的美麗。




The "Amphion"

  This ship was designed as a fast ship for the use and enjoyment of Gustave III of Sweden.  But she was also used as a warship in the Battle of Svenksund.  It was from this ship that the king directed the victorious war against the Russian Empire.

  The "Amphion" was built in the shipyard of Djurgarden (Stockholm), launched in 1778, and was well-known until 1850.  The original plan of this ship, a masterpiece of harmony and purity of lines, was done by Fredrik of Chapman, the first naval architect who codified the planning of the hull of the ship based on precise mathematical studies, and not only on empirical data as was usual at the time.

  Stockholm has preserved in her Statens Sjohistoriska a model of the "Amphion".  The model there shows that the ship was a "schooner", with a pointed hull which gave her an exceptional naval quality.

  The model presented here is based on the original documents and made in the scale of 1 to 40.  Particular efforts have been made to show her decorations of gold-gilded metals while different woods are used to reflect the beautiful combination of colors and veinings of the ship.
