勝利號

  這艘船造於1765年，全長176呎（53.64公尺），深51.5呎（15.7公尺）可載船員850人，並載有104門大砲。

1805年10月21日英國海軍提督納爾遜元帥（Horatio Nelson）乘這艘旗艦於特拉法爾加戰役（Trafalgar）打敗法國和西班牙聯合艦隊，確立英國制海權，並擊碎了拿破崙欲征服世界之美夢。 為紀念此艦之光輝歷史，英國人將她保存於樸茨茅斯（Portsmouth）港，做為海事博物館供人參觀。




The "Victory"

  Built in 1765, this warship was 176 feet long, and 51.5 feet deep.  She carried 850 crew and 104 main battery guns.

  It was on this flagship that Admiral Horatio Nelson defeated the combined French and Spanish fleets in the Battle of Trafalgar on Oct. 21, 1805, ending Napoleon I's dream of conquest in the East and establishing for England her domination of the seas.  In memory of her glorious history, the people of England preserve the "Victory" in Portsmouth harbor as a maritime museum.
