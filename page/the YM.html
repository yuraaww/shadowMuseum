瑪麗號遊艇

  此船是荷蘭為東印度公司於1660年所建造。 阿姆斯特丹市隨即將此船贈予英國國王查理二世，約有一年的時間，她被英國海軍當作軍艦使用，其後才提供政界、財界的重要人物當作沿岸航行的工具。 1675年，因觸礁而沉沒。

  1971年一群考古學家與造船專家受聘調查船難原因。 他們撈起殘骸，加以研判，並使她恢復原狀。 此消息廣傳於愛好遊艇的人們口中，隔年，26艘同樣的船就被複製出來。




The "Yacht Mary"

  Built by the Netherlands for the East India Company in 1660, this ship was soon presented by Amsterdam as a gift to Charles II of the United Kingdom, and was used by the British navy for about a year as a battleship. Then she was used for coastal sailing by politicians and business tycoons. In 1675 she sank after striking a reef.

  In 1971, a group of archeologists and experts in shipbuilding were hired to investigate the cause of the shipwreck. They dredged up the remains, examined and restored them into the original shape of the ship.

  After the news was spread among yacht lovers, 26 replicas of the ship were made in her memory the next year.
