大哈利號

  這艘1000噸的大哈利號，是英王亨利八世面對西班牙日漸壯大的威脅，於1514年建造的。 這艘船代表該世紀所擁有的最佳海艦科技。 船上有鋒利的武器。 全體船員都有各式各樣的步槍和手榴彈裝備。 大哈利號不僅是一艘船，更是一座堡壘，因為當時，士兵比水手多而且重要。




Great Harry

  The 1,000-ton "Great Harry" was built by Henry VIII of England in 1514 to meet the growing threat of Spain.  She represented the finest expression of naval technology available in the era.  She had a formidable armament.  Her crew were armed with a variety of muskets and grenades.

  The "Great Harry" was more a fortress than a ship, for soldiers were more numerous and important than sailors at that time.
