密蘇里戰艦

  美國密蘇里號戰艦「Mighty Mo」是第四艘以「唯我維揚(Show Me)」州名命名的第二艘軍艦。 密蘇里號戰艦(USS Missouri BB-63)為愛荷華級戰艦的第三艘，這艘58,000噸愛荷華級戰艦於1941年安裝龍骨，在第二次世界大戰即將結束的1944年1月水，提供二次大戰間琉磺島與沖繩兩大戰役所需的炮火，並沒有立下赫赫戰功，可是機遇好，1945年8月15日，日本天皇宣布無條件投降時，密蘇里號戰艦正在日本東京灣附近巡戈，8月19日駛進了東京灣，在1945年9月2日擔任日本戰敗的受降艦而聞名於世，各參戰的代表們輪流簽署這份投降文件，如此結束了一次歷史上最血腥的大戰。

  1950年6月下旬韓戰爆發後，本艦隨即前往韓國戰場，並以其16吋主砲為在半島上浴血奮戰的美軍與聯合國部隊提供火力支援，韓戰結束後，本艦於1955年2月除役封存在華盛頓州布利莫頓普吉灣海軍造船廠中。 密蘇里軍艦無所事事的停在布利莫頓廠時，據估計一年有18萬人以上曾在這歷史性的柚木甲板參觀。

  1986年5月，因雷根總統的600艘作戰艦艇的大艦隊計劃而復役，並進行現代化改裝工程，使其可發射「戰斧」巡弋飛彈及「魚叉」反艦飛彈，然後在該年9月至12月間環遊世界作試航性的巡航，這是自1907到1909年之間老羅斯福總統的「偉大白色艦隊」以來，第一艘環球航行的軍艦，歸航後停靠在加州長堤。

  1987年密蘇里號軍艦航向多事的波斯灣海域，支援荷莫茲海峽附近的航運工作。  1988年間，參與夏威夷海岸的太平洋沿岸演習。  1989年初，例行的造船廠維修期後，密蘇里號軍艦回到海上，同年年末參加太平洋演習，並拜訪韓國釜山。  1991年1月17日，本艦投入波灣戰爭，除對陸上的伊拉克軍隊發射巡戈飛彈外，還在對科威特發動的兩棲佯攻中，擔任岸轟的工作。  1992年第二次，也是最後一次除役並成為紀念艦，1998年移至珍珠港，1999年1月29日開放供遊人參觀。




Missouri Battleship

  USS Missouri, "Mighty Mo", is the fourth ship and the second battleship to bear the name of the "Show Me" state.  The ship is also the last Iowa-class battleship built by the United States.

  The keel for the 58,000 tons Iowa-class battleship was laid in 1941 and MISSOURI was launched in 1944.  The ship provided gunfire support in the battles of Iwo Jima and Okinawa during World War II.  On September 2, 1945, BATTLESHIP MISSOURI was the spot for the signing of a Formal Instrument of Surrender by Japan to the Allied Forces, ending World War II.  As the mighty dreadnought sat at anchor in Tokyo Bay, Japan, representatives from the warring nations each took turns signing the Surrender, thus ending one of the bloodiest wars in history.

  MISSOURI was called on to participate in several shore bombardments off the coast of Korea between 1950 and 1953 before being decommissioned and placed in "mothballs" at Puget Sound Naval Shipyard in Bremerton, Washington in 1955.  It was estimated that more than 180,000 people a year walked the historic teak wood decks white MISSOURI sat idle in Bremerton.

  MISSOURI was recommissioned in 1986 in San Francisco, California and departed on an Around-the-World Shakedown cruise--the first battleship to circumnavigate the world since President Theodore Roosevelt's "Great White Fleet" of 1907-1909.

  In 1987, MISSOURI journeyed to the troubled waters of the Persian Gulf, supporting shipping operations near the Strait of Hormuz.  During 1988, MISSOURI participated in the Rim of the Pacific (RimPac) Exercise off the coast of Hawaii.  Following a routine shipyard period in early 1989, MISSOURI returned to sea and later in the year participated in Pacific Exercise (PacEx)'89 and visited Pusan, Korea.  As for the 1990's, MISSOURI is as she was during the 1940's:"ready for sea and always ready to answer the call of battle."

  BATTLESHIP MISSOURI was decommissioned on March 31, 1992 for the second and final time.  The ship is homeported in Long Beach, California.  She now serves as a memorial and museum.
