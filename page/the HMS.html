由尼肯號

  由尼肯號記載於查布曼有名的著作「船舶備忘錄」中(1768年發行)。 根據18世紀驅逐艦之紀錄，在砲臺甲板與後甲板上裝載了32門9磅的砲。

  驅逐艦是在帆船時代各國海軍競相建造之船艦，有巡邏、搜索船團及護衛等多種用途。

  驅逐艦的航速勝過戰列艦，火砲次於戰列艦，因此她常被航行以完成任務。




The "H. M. S. Unicorn"

  This warship was built in England in the 18th century according to Chapman's well-known book, A Memorandum of Ships, published in 1768.  Information about destroyers of that century indicates that this battleship was mounted with 32 9-pound guns on the gun deck and the quarter deck.

  Destroyers of this kind, a battleship which the navies of all nations competed to build in great numbers in the age of sailboats, had such functions as escorting ships, ocean cruising and searching for a fleet.  Destroyers surpass fighting ships in speed, though they are not so powerful in guns.  They, therefore, are often dispatched to achieve ocean-crossing missions.
