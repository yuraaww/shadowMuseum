聖•菲力浦號

  此船於1693年在西班牙下水，她是一艘三層甲板的一級戰艦，長287呎（87.5公尺），寬54呎(16.5公尺)，吃水22呎(6.7公尺)。 主甲板上有50門32磅規格砲，中甲板有30門24磅規格砲，上甲板上有22門18磅及8門9磅規格砲。 這艘裝備104門炮的堅強戰艦卻從未獻身戰陣。

  事實上，1588年無敵艦隊被英國海軍打敗時，西班牙已失去制海權，有如落日西沉，海軍一蹶不振，這艘船的建造只能代表一種欲求海上霸權的最後掙扎而已。 不過，這艘船卻頗見工藝，船身內外有雙重照明設備，結構耐久；大砲是以昂貴的青銅製成，兩個大錨各有八噸重，小錨也各有四噸重。 除外，和戰爭無關的各部分都依西班牙的習慣，裝飾極為富麗。

  這艘船堅固華麗，卻在1736年被當成無用之物處理，我們把它陳列在本館，因為她象徵一艘戰艦的興衰起落。

  本模型船是按1:80比例製造完成。




The "San Philip"

  This model was made in a scale of 1 to 80 according to the ship built in 1693 in Spain, a first-rate triple-decked battleship which was 287 feet in length, 54 feet in breadth, with a draft of 22 feet.

  On the main deck were mounted 50 32-pound standardized guns; on the tween deck, 30 24-pound standardized guns, and on the upper deck, 22 18-pound and 8 9-pound standardized guns.  This able-bodied ship armed with 104 guns, however, was never engaged in battle.

  In fact, in 1588, when the Armada was defeated by the English navy, Spain lost power over the sea, fading as the setting sun.  With her navy declining, the construction of this ship merely meant one last struggle for supremacy on the sea. This ship, nevertheless, showed craftsmanship.  The hull, inside and outside, had double lighting; the structure was endurable; the guns were made of expensive bronze; each of the two large anchors weighed 8 tons and each of the small ones, 4 tons.  Besides, all the parts of the ship which had nothing to do with fighting were richly decorated following Spanish custom.

  Stalwart and richly ornamented as this ship was, she was treated as worthless in 1736.  We display her model here, for she symbolizes the rise and fall of a warship tradition.
