鄭和寶船

  鄭和寶船在600年前，是世界上最大的海船，比哥倫布在80多年後跨越大西洋發現美洲之聖瑪麗亞號還大約十倍。 據文獻記載，長度達一百二十五公尺，寬達五十二公尺，是一艘九桅十二帆的木造帆船，設備完善，裝飾豪華有如水上皇宮。 船中滿載「寶貨」—去程時載金銀及中國產物，以備賞賜外國君主；回程時在海外的奇珍異寶，故稱寶船。

鄭和(Cheng Ho，西元1371-1435)，15世紀中國航海家、外交家。本姓馬，小字三保，回族，雲南昆陽(今併入晉寧縣)人，祖、父均曾到伊斯蘭聖地麥加朝聖，故幼時了解外洋情況。 1381年明軍入雲南時被俘入燕王府為宦官，後成為燕王朱棣親信，1399年燕王「靖難」，監軍有功，賜姓鄭，升內官監太監。 1405年奉命通使「西洋」率大小艦船百餘艘27,800人出長江口南下，兩年而返，前後七次奉使，歷時廿八年，先後到達東南亞、印度半島、波斯灣、阿拉伯半島、非洲東岸和紅海口，訪問三十餘國。 迎送各國使節，促進了中國與各國的經濟文化交流。在世界航海史上，鄭和的遠航比哥倫布(1492年)早87年，比伽馬(1498年)早93年，比麥哲倫(1521年)早116年。 本船比例1:100。




Cheng Ho Treasure Boat

  Cheng Ho(1371-1435), a Chinese navigator and diplomat in the 15th century, was a Moslem born in the K'un-Yang County of Yun-Nan Province.  His ancestors used to make pilgrimage to Meca, so foreign affairs were not new to him since childhood.  With the invasion of the Ming army into Yun-Nan in 1381, Cheng Ho was captured and then enlisted as an officer and finally became a confidant of Emperor Yen.  In 1399, his military merit enabled him to be promoted to be an officer of high prestige, and the surname of Cheng was bestowed on him by the Emperor.  In 1405, Cheng Ho was commissioned an ambassador to western countries, leading a fleet of more than one hundred ships and boats and a crew of 27,800.  Going southward from the mouth of the Yang-tze River and then returning to China in two years, Cheng had accomplished seven missions within twenty-eight years.  He had visited more than thirty nations in Southeast Asia, Indian Peninsula, Persian Gulf, Arabian Peninsula, East Africa, and the Red Sea, greatly enhancing the economic and cultural exchanges between China and those nations.  In the history of world navigation, the record of Cheng Ho's navigation was 87 years earlier than that of Columbus (1492), 93 years earlier than that of Gama(1498), and 116 years earlier than that of Magellan(1521).
