富士丸

  日本三菱造船廠於1937年3月建造完成，行駛於日本神戶至台灣基隆間定期航線最豪華客輪，載客頭等艙31名、二等艙165名、三等艙781名。

  該輪於1943年10月27日太平洋戰爭中從基隆駛返神戶航行中，在九州南方奄美大島附近遭美軍淺水艇魚雷擊沉。




The "Fuji Maru"

PASSENGER LINER : TAIWAN ROUTE BUILDER : MITSUBISHI DOCKYARD NAGASAKI LAUNCH : MARCH 15,1937 MAIN ENGINE TYPE : 2 STEAM TURBOS OUTPUT RATING : 12,167 RPM TOP SPEED : 19.9 KNOTS SPEED/HR : 17.0 KNOTS TONS GROSS : 9,138 LENGTH : 146.0 M DRAUGHT : 7.3 M BREADTH : 18. 4 M DEPTH : 11.0 M

  Passenger vessel built at Mitsubishi Dockyard, Nagasaki, Japan to provide deluxe passenger serve between Kobe, Japan and Keelung, Taiwan.  Fuji Maru accommodated 31 passengers in first class, 165 second class and 781 third class.

  She was torpedoed by an American submarine on its way back to Kobe on October 27, 1943 and sunk finally nearby western Amami (South of Kyushu).
