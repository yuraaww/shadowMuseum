瑪麗皇后號

  這艘船是康納德輪船公司在1926年所構想，由蘇格蘭布朗公司所建造。 1930年12月1日第一個螺絲釘釘上龍骨。然後因受世界不景氣影響，所有的工程停頓下來，直到1934年4月3日才復工。 1934年9月26日，瑪麗皇后陛下為她命名；當她的巨大船身在克來德河下水之時，有20萬人為她喝采。

  這艘船共81,237噸，1019.05英呎（310.74公尺）長，可維持每小時28.05浬（55.17公里）的速度。 從1936年5月27日自英國首航載運了2,148位客人以後數年，這艘郵輪可說是世界上最大且最快的一艘輪船。

  她不但是最大郵輪，同時也曾扮演別條船未曾扮演過的角色-包括囚船、醫療船、戰時新娘及嬰兒的運送船及軍艦等。 當她扮演軍艦時，在二次大戰戰事吃緊之際，穿梭美歐兩州，補給物資及人員，她曾航行了60萬哩，載運了近90萬男男女女，而且從未在敵人炮火下失掉過旅客，雖然德、意、日等國一直設法想炸沉她，是艘世界最幸運的輪船。 扮演完多重角色後，這艘壯麗郵輪目前已莊嚴地停靠在加州長堤，她不僅是擁有精緻旅客和商店的客棧聚會中心，亦是南加州眾人喜愛的勝景。

The "Queen Mary"

  This ship was conceived in 1926 by the Board of Directors of the Cunard Steamship Company and built by John Brown & Company Scotland.  On Dec. 1, 1930, the first rivet was driven into the keel.  All work, however, stopped due to the Great Depression one year later and did not resume until April 3, 1934.  On September 26, 1934, she was christened by her Majesty Queen Mary and was cheered by over 200,000 spectators as her mammoth hull slid into the River Clyde.

  Weighing 81, 237 tons, measuring 1,019.05 feet (310.74m) and maintaining a cruising speed of 28.05 knots (55.17km/hr), this glamorous ocean liner was the largest and fastest ship in the world for many years after she sailed from England on her maiden voyage carrying 2,148 passengers, officers, and crew on May 27,1936.

  In addition to being the largest passenger liner, this ship played many roles other ships never played.  These include prison ship, troopship, hospital ship, and transport for war brides and babies.  As a troopship she sailed over 600,000 miles, transported nearly 900,000 men and women, and never lost a passenger to enemy fire, though Germany, Italy and Japan sought to destroy her.  After playing so many roles, this magnificent liner now rests majestically in her permanent berth at Long Beach, California, serving as a most elegant hotel-convention center with fine restaurants and shops and as a major California attraction.

  This model was built in the scale of 1 to 150.
