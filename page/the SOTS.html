海霸王號

  海霸王是英國國王查理一世建造的大型帆船，於1637年下水。 它的雕工精美裝飾了很多的人像且鑲上金箔，非常華麗。 擁有三根船桅、三層甲板與壯觀的巨砲，領先它的時代約150年。 由於它身經百戰，所向無敵且行駛在海上會閃閃發光，荷蘭水兵稱它金色的魔鬼。




Sovereign of the Seas

  This ship was launched in 1637.  She was larger and more powerful than any other ships of her time.  This ornately carved and gilded, three-masted, three-decked warship, with her impressive armament, was said to be 150 years in advance of her time.  She fought many battles, during which she was never defeated.  The Dutch seamen called her "The Golden Devil".
