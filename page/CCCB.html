鄭成功中軍船

  鄭成功中軍船的模型，是根據廈門督造軍艦的記載，設計而成的一艘福船型的中軍船，即指揮艦，三桅三帆、木質結構，船尾樓點將台邊配有一把涼傘、一面帥旗，船尾設有一盞宮燈，主桅頂有一面長條形彩旗和風向儀，甲板上配有火炮十四門。

  鄭成功(Cheng Cheng-Kung，西元1624.8.28-1662.6.23)，中國明清之際收復台灣的民族英雄，名森，字大木。 福建南安人，出生於日本平戶，1630年七歲時回到中國，1645年南明隆武帝(朱聿鍵)賜姓朱，改名成功，尊為「國姓爺」，後被永歷帝封為延平郡王。 1646年清軍進攻南明隆武政權。成功反對其父鄭芝龍不戰而降，在福建沿海不斷與清軍作戰，1659年率大軍十萬人攻入長江，進圍南京，1661年4月率軍隊二萬五千人進軍台灣，經九個多月的奮戰後，於1662年2月1日從荷蘭侵略者手中收復台灣。 在台灣建立行政機構，屯田開墾、發展貿易，促進了台灣社會經濟的發展。本船比例1:25。




Cheng Ch'eng-Kung Chung-Chun Boat

  Cheng Ch'eng-Kung(1624-1662), the national hero who recovered Taiwan from the hands of Hollanders at the time of the late Ming dynasty and the early Ch'ing dynasty, was a descendant of Nan-an County of Fu chien Province.  Born in Japan, Cheng returned to China in 1630 at the age of seven.  In 1645, the Lung Wu Emperor of the Southern Ming dynasty granted him with the surname of Chu and renamed him as Ch'eng-Kung with the respectful address of "Koxinga".  He was later bestowed by Yung Li Emperor as the ruler of Yen P'ing County.  In 1646, the Ch'ing army launched military attacks on the Southern Ming dynasty.  Enraged by his father's surrender without fighting, Cheng started fighting with the Ch'ing army along the coast of Fu-chien Province during the following years.  He led an army of 100,000 deep into the Yang-tze River and besieged Nan-Ching City in 1659.  In April 1661, Cheng was leading an army of 25,000 to Taiwan and, after nine months of combat, regained Taiwan from the hands of Dutch invaders on February 1, 1662.  Soon, Cheng established the administration institution, engaged in farming exploration and trade development, and thus promoted the social and economic development of Taiwan. Scale 1:25
