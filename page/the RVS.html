皇家維京海洋號

  這艘屬皇家維京航運公司的客輪，定期在阿拉斯加和加拿大之間航行。 重28,000噸，長205公尺，可載710名旅客。 這艘船曾在1982年改裝。

  她的姊妹船叫皇家維京太陽號，建於1985年。 該船是世界上最漂亮，設備最完備之客輪。 重36,000噸，長205公尺，寬28.9公尺，可載客740名。




The "Royal Viking Sea"

  This ship, a Norwegian passenger liner regularly cruising the Alaska-Canada route, is owned by the Royal Viking Line.  A vessel of 28,000 gross tons, she is 205 meters long, and can accommodate 710 passengers.  She was remodelled in 1982.

  Her sister ship, the "Royal Viking Sun", was built in 1985.  She is the world's most graceful and best-equipped passenger liner.  Being 36,000 gross tons, 205 meters long, and 28.9 meters wide, she accommodates 740 passengers.
