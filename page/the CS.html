卡提薩爾克號

  18世紀初，歐洲各國的茶葉貿易甚為興盛，從中國、印度和斯里蘭卡等地由最快速度的帆船(Clippers，亦稱tea clippers)運送，卡提薩爾克(Cutty Sark)便是最快速的帆船之一。 造於1869年，最高時速是17.5浬(約32公里)，一日航程曾創下363浬(約669公里)的速度，在104天內完成了從倫敦到上海的處女航。

  1954年12月10日移到格林威治(Greenwich)特殊乾船塢，為英國國家紀念帆船，她是唯一殘存的快速帆船(clipper)。

  船的長度212呎(64.6公尺) ，寬度36呎(10.9公尺) ，深度(甲板下)21呎(6.4公尺) ，總噸數912噸。 是以良質的堅材與鐵混合建造，尤其吃水部分，以銅片貼合以利增快速度。




The "Cutty Sark"

  In the early eighteenth century when the tea trade to Europe was flourishing via deliveries made by clippers or tea clippers which sailed at their fastest speeds from China, India, or SriLanka, the "Cutty Sark", built in 1869, was among the fastest sailing ships at that time.  Her top speed was 17.5 knots (32km/hr), having made a record of 363 nautical miles in one day.  She completed her maiden voyage from London to Shanghai in 104 days.

  On December 10, 1954, she was moved to a special dock in Greenwich and is kept there as a heritage sailboat of the United Kingdom, being the only clipper which has ever survived to the present time.

  The "Cutty Sark" is 212 feet long, 36 feet wide, 21 feet deep(as measured from the deck), and 912 gross tons. She was made of good hard timbers and iron.  Her bottom below water was coated with sheets of copper to help increase her sailing speed.
